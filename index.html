About

Adam Waito (he/him) is a multidisciplinary artist based in the west end of Toronto with over a decade of experience as a professional Illustrator and Graphic Designer and nearly a decade in the animation industry as a 2D Background Artist, Layout Supervisor, and occasional Animator.


Always looking for new creative challenges, Adam has more recently taken on Web Development and Design (see: this website). In a past life, he was a pretty busy Montreal-based musician touring internationally in struggling indie rock bands, and he still releases and performs music under the moniker Sparkling Water.
